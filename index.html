Neat is a effective approach for applying one of main algorithms of evolutionary computation (genetic algorithm) on nueral networks in which narrow networks with few nodes on their layers gradully evolve and become more deep with more nodes. because of this process of gradully updateding they are capable of searching broad range of solutions in solution space. also because of operators like mutation which are applied to members in population through evolution on genetic algorithm, they can avoid stacking in local optimom solution and this can led to finding solution that gradient based algorithms don't have chance to reach
